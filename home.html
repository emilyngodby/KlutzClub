Klutz Club

Wonky... wonky!

Join the Klutz Club and show off your graceless skills in low-stakes mini-games against an equally amateur opponent.
Feedback Form | Play Game

Klutz Club is an arcade-style game set in a carnival where a player can customize their avatar and participate in a number of different mini-games.

Upon entry to the game, the player will be in a lobby where they can customize their character and get to know how its avatar moves.

There will be multiple doors in the lobby in a fun-house style (different sizes, shapes, ways to open).

Each door is an entrance into different mini-games. The mini-games have unexpected controls and the player movement is awkward and unpredictable.

The player competes in each mini-game and is also given a score that is collected on a leaderboard to compete against other players.
